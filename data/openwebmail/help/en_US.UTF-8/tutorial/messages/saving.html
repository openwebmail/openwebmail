OpenWebMail Help Tutorials >> Saving A Message

You may want to type up a message, and send it later. This can be done by saving a Draft. It will save all of the header information (To, From, Subject, etc.) as well as the message text. Once you have finished typing your message, click "Save Draft" and your message will be stored in the "Draft" folder. To restore your message and send it later, simply find it in your "Draft" folder (see Managing Your Folders ), select the message by clicking on it, open it with the "Edit Draft" button (  ) and then click "Send". You can also edit it before sending.
