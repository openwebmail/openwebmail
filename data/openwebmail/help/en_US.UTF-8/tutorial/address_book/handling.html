OpenWebMail Help Tutorials >> Handling several Address Books

OpenWebMail easily manages several address books. Click on adress books icon ( ) to handle your addressbooks.

You will be redirected to the Edit Adressbooks window. In this window you can create, delete, rename and dowload your address books.

A special address book named Global Addressbook may have been created by your System Administrator. You can not rename or delete this special address book.
